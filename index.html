Test your geography knowledge with our three level quiz

How To Play


Click "Let's Play!" to begin the game. There are three levels, each containing ten questions, for a total of thirty multiple choice questions. You must beat the current level to move onto the next level, or else you will have to start over.


How to beat the levels:

  • Level 1: Score a total of 6 points
  • Level 2: Score a total of 12 points
  • Level 3: Score a total of 20 points

Good luck!
